Meet Our Products

Regular Aroma

Our regular coffee is darkly colored, bitter, slightly acidic and has a stimulating effect in humans, primarily due to its caffeine content.

Mocha

A mixture of coffee and chocolate that contains chocolate flavor (Top flavor on earth). It is generally sweet and is usually served with a layer of milk on top.

Hazel

A taste of flavored coffee that is distinctive and somewhat tropical. A predominant Hazelnut flavor and subtle background notes of coconut.

Dolce

Dolce means italian for "sweet". The sweets of cinnamon, butter and brown sugar evoke handcrafted desserts—flavors that are nicely balanced by the mellow softness of the lighter roasted blend flavor pack to these beans.

Vanilla

It uses basic ingredients - just regular brew coffee, vanilla sugar or extract, milk, and ice. It's delicious and refreshing and everything an ice coffee should be.

Caramel

Combination of brewed coffee and warmed milk infused with caramel and brown sugar pack flavor.
